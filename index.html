The Leaders of Tomorrow Program

Our Leaders of Tomorrow program consists of ten-modules, designed to teach life-long leadership skills to young people. Each module has a unique focus on confidence, team-building, goal-setting, and self-expression. We hold participants of the Leaders of Tomorrow Club to a very high standard. We have high expectations for their behavior, attitude, and engagement in the program. Our classes are designed to help young adults become more confident in school, university, their career, and life.
